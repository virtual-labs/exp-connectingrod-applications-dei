### Procedure
**1.** Observe the given model of the connecting rod and identify the **small end,** **big end,**          and **shank.**  

**2.** Study how the small end connects to the piston using a **gudgeon pin.**  

**3.** Observe the attchment of the **big end** to the crankshaft at the **crankpin journal.**  

**4.** Analyze the **H-section** and **I-section** design of the connecting rod and note how it            improvs stregth.  

**5.** Observe the **grain flow direction** in the rod body(vertical) and cap(horizontal).  

**6.** Study the mateial used in the connectig rod construction and their application in different         engines.  

**7.** Identify possible **failure modes** such as "throwing a rod" due to fatigue or lubrication          failure.
