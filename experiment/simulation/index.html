Connecting Rod Simulation Lab

Simulation Setup – Part 1

Description: Connecting rod forming process comprises billet compression and deformation by closed impression die forming process. The material of the billet is manganese alloy steel and maintaining temperature of billet to 1250°C. Hydraulic press is used to deform the billet into a semi-finished shape.

Rod Strain – Part 1

Description: This simulation displays the variation of equivalent strain during the initial forming phase. High deforming force and temperature cause the material to flow plastically into the die impressions.

Simulation Setup – Part 2

Description: Continuation of the forging process under controlled parameters. The video shows the transition from the intermediate preform to the final semi-finished connecting rod.

Rod Strain – Part 2

Description: Final strain analysis showing the finished connecting rod. The strain becomes more uniform, indicating that the grain refinement is complete and the mechanical properties are optimized.
